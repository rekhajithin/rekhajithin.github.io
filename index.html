My Initiative - The Acharya Foundation

In loving memory of my beloved father Acharya Adinadhan

To continue the legacy of my beloved father Acharya Adinadhan who selflessly served the community with his medical practice, I conduct free medical camps to help underprivilaged people manage and reverse chronic diseases with Ayurveda.
All patients get free consultations, basic health checks, medicines and lifestyle plans. The camps are conducted every second Saturday of the month at Acharya Aryurveda & Wellness Centre, Trivandrum, India.
If you know someone sufferring from diseases like diabetes, cardiovascuslar diseases, cancer or chronic pain and is not able to afford treatment, please feel free to contact me.
