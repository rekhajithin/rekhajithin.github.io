My Initiative - The Acharya Foundation

To continue the legacy of my beloved father Acharya Adinadhan who selflessly served the community, I conduct free medical camps to help underprivilaged people manage and reverse chronic diseases with Ayurveda.

All patients get free consultation, health check, medicines and lifestyle plan. The camps are conducted every second Saturday of the month at Acharya Aryurvedic Centre, Trivandrum, India.

If you know someone sufferring from diseases like Diabetes, Cardiovascuslar diseases, Cancer or Chronic Pain and is not able to afford treatment, please feel free to contact us.
