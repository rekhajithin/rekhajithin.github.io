Reversal of Diabetes

Yes! You heard that right. Reversal of Diabetes!

During the initial years of my practice as an Ayurvedic doctor, right after my medical school, I have tried 'treating', rather 'managing' Type II Diabetes with medicines. To be very honest, it was just like instilling manure on the top of a plant which has already grown big to a tree! I used to meet the same patients month after month coming for the prescription to restock their medicines. Little did I know that I was not "treating" but just "managing" a disease. Over the years, after a lot of learning and of course 'unlearning' along the way, I have come to strongly belive in the existence of what I call as "reversal" of Diabetes. Yes! Its not magic but just lifestyle!

The focus is on Four Pillars of Health- Sleep, Relaxation, Exercise and Nutrition. With these Four Pillars along with the science of Ayurveda, the reversal of Diabetes is possible!
